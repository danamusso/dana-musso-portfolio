About

Dana Musso is a graduate of the University of Miami that received a BFA in Graphic Design with minors in Art History and Advertising. Dana wears a lot of different hats (as pictured) and works as a multidisciplinary artist, taking on commissions in graphic design, painting, and embroidered artworks. As a driven and passionate creator, Dana is always seeking more opportunities to grow. Born and raised in sunny, South Florida, her work is heavily inspired by her tropical surroundings and sunny energy. Art has always been Dana’s passion, and loves when she has the chance to share it with others! She is believes that taking on new challenges enable her to perfect her skills and bettering her craft. Dana’s artistic skills stretch from graphic design, web design, illustration, painting, and fibers. Dana believes there is always more to learn and is a big proponent of continuing her education in the arts. Dana’s work aims to intertwine mystical motifs with modernity while remaining light hearted.
